Bikeio is a nonprofit organization founded in Boynton Beach, Florida, in 2016. Our mission is to give a sense of community to fellow humans who travel using a bicycle. It does not matter where you go or what you're doing, as we believe in enabling and inspiring people to ride.

Since 2018, Bikeio has given more than a million dollars worth of prizes and has donated to charities to children's hospitals across the world dedicated to encouraging this lifestyle.
